# Code of Conduct

We want DriftGrid to be a welcoming place for designers, developers, and people who like grid views of HTML.

## In short

- Be kind.
- Assume good intent.
- Critique work, not people.
- Don't harass, intimidate, or discriminate.
- Keep discussions relevant and constructive.

## If something goes wrong

Report concerns to **jeff@bzydesign.com**. Reports stay private. We'll acknowledge within 72 hours and follow up with whatever steps make sense — a conversation, a warning, a block, whatever the situation needs.

Unacceptable behavior includes (but is not limited to): harassment, personal attacks, sustained disruption, publishing others' private information, and deliberately unwelcoming behavior toward contributors.

## Scope

This applies across GitHub issues and PRs, discussions, Discord, email, and any official DriftGrid spaces.

---

Based loosely on the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/). Shortened for readability — the spirit is the same.
