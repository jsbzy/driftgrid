Three Lanes of Healthcare AI
1
Lane One
Provider-Facing Clinical Support
Tools that sit behind licensed clinicians. The clinician remains present, directs care, and retains responsibility. The AI functions as a productivity multiplier within existing professional oversight.
Regulatory: Exempt under §520(o)(1)(E)
Abridge, Nuance, Rad AI, OpenEvidence, UpToDate
2
Lane Two
Consumer-Facing Health & Wellness AI
Systems that help individuals interpret health information and arrive at clinical encounters better prepared. They include explicit disclaimers and are designed to stay wide of the regulatory line.
Regulatory: Disclaimers, not authorization
OpenAI, Anthropic, Amazon health assistants
3
Lane Three
Regulated, Patient-Facing Clinical AI
Physician-prescribed AI inside the care pathway. When the end point of the clinical interaction is a patient, the clinician’s license is no longer standing behind the output. FDA authorization provides the accountability structure.
Regulatory: Meets device definition — De Novo pathway
