Why Post-Operative Recovery First
01
Bounded scope makes validation possible
A specific procedure and a finite recovery window. Two hard boundaries that make it possible to build the clinical validation harnesses the FDA requires for a De Novo submission.
02
Decades of clinical literature to validate against
Recovery protocols for pain, mobility, sleep, diet, and wound healing — dense, procedure-specific, and stress-tested by practitioners. The evidence base AI recommendations can be measured against.
03
The clinical need is genuine and measurable
50% of patients forget discharge guidance within two weeks. 40% of readmissions are tied to missed early signs. $60B in annual complications — the predictable cost of an interval that has never had adequate supervision.
