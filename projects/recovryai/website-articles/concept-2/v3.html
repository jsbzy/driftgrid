Three Lanes of Healthcare AI
Lane One
Provider-Facing Clinical Support
AI behind the clinician. The physician remains present, directs care, and retains responsibility.
Regulatory
Exempt under §520(o)
Lane Two
Consumer-Facing Health & Wellness AI
AI in the consumer’s hand. Useful for health literacy, but carries no clinical accountability.
Regulatory
Disclaimers, not authorization
Lane Three
Regulated, Patient-Facing Clinical AI
AI prescribed by the physician, used by the patient. FDA authorization provides the accountability structure.
