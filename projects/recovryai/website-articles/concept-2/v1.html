Three Lanes of Healthcare AI
Lane One
Provider-Facing Clinical Support
End User
Licensed clinician
Regulatory Posture
Exempt under §520(o)(1)(E) — clinician reviews & retains judgment
Accountability
Clinician’s license stands behind every output
Abridge, Nuance, Rad AI, OpenEvidence, UpToDate
Lane Two
Consumer-Facing Health & Wellness AI
End User
Consumer / patient (outside care pathway)
Regulatory Posture
Designed to stay wide of the regulatory line — disclaimers, not authorization
Accountability
No clinical accountability — useful, and dangerous in proportion to drift
OpenAI, Anthropic, Amazon health assistants, symptom checkers
Lane Three
Regulated, Patient-Facing Clinical AI
End User
Patient (inside care pathway, physician-prescribed)
Regulatory Posture
Meets device definition — FDA authorization via De Novo pathway
Accountability
Cleared indication, evidence record, defined safety architecture
