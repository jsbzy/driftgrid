# RecovryAI — Brand Guidelines

## About
FDA-Authorized Virtual Care Assistants for Post-Operative Recovery. AI-powered platform that guides patients through recovery with personalized, clinically-validated care protocols.

## Logo
Medical cross + AI sparkle mark. Horizontal lockup is primary. Stacked alternate for square formats.
- Use `logos/logo-color-dark.svg` on dark backgrounds
- Use `logos/logo-color-light.svg` on light backgrounds
- Use `logos/logo-white.svg` reversed on dark
- Use `logos/logo-black.svg` for mono applications
- Icon-only: `logos/icon-pulse.svg`, `logos/icon-black.svg`, `logos/icon-white.svg`
- Mark only: `logos/rxai-mark.svg`

## Typography
- Primary: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar geometric sans)
- Wordmark uses custom letterforms — lowercase "recovry.ai"
- Hierarchy: bold headlines, regular body, light captions
- Prefer sentence case for headlines

## Voice & Tone
- Clinical authority meets human warmth
- Confident but not cold — accessible to patients and providers
- Key phrases: "virtual care assistant", "post-operative recovery", "FDA approved"
- Avoid jargon-heavy medical language when addressing patients

## Reference
- Website: recovry.ai
- Positioning: "Every patient prescribed a virtual care assistant"
- Products: iOS app, Apple Watch companion, provider dashboard
