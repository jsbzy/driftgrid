04 / AGENT INTERACTION
Two ways for the patient
to interact with
the assistant.
The VCA engages patients in two key ways. First, it initiates daily check-ins: structured assessments covering pain, sleep, medication, diet, and activity. Second, patients can reach out anytime with questions or concerns. Both modes feed the same clinical intelligence, ensuring nothing falls through the cracks during unsupervised recovery at home.
