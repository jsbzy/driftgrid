Key UX Experiences & Principles

A UX exploration for physician-prescribed AI in post-operative recovery
THE GAP

80% of surgeries are now same-day or outpatient. Patients come home with little more than a PDF. The 90 days that follow, the hardest part of recovery, happen largely alone. RecovryAI fills that gap as a physician-prescribed Virtual Care Assistant that monitors, guides, and acts on behalf of the patient from Day 1 to Day 90 and beyond.

DESIGN FOUNDATIONS

Four Primitives

Before individual moments, we established the foundational design language:

Conversation-First
Chat-based at its core. Works inside any messaging environment. As few touches as possible.
Embeddable Cards
Structured information as embeddable, portable cards. The atom of the RecovryAI experience.travels wherever the patient is.
Rx Trust Mark
Visual proof on every VCA message that this is physician-prescribed, FDA-pathway AI. Like the lock icon in your browser.small, ubiquitous, essential.
Cognitive Gentleness
The lens we evaluate every design through. Assumes the patient at their lowest capacity.
Cognitive Gentleness

Post-surgery patients are on pain medication, exhausted, and often anxious. Every interaction assumes the patient at their lowest cognitive capacity. One question at a time. Large touch targets. Simple, warm language.never clinical jargon. The VCA feels like a calm, steady presence.not a system demanding input. This isn't a feature. It's a lens we apply to every patient-facing moment.
