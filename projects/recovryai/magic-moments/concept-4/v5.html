HOW DIMENSIONS BECOME STATUS
AGGREGATION APPROACHES

Worst dimension wins: If any dimension hits red, overall is red. Simple, conservative. But one bad night of sleep could trigger a yellow when everything else is fine.

Weighted composite: Pain and Mobility weigh heavier than Nutrition. A weighted average determines the tier. More nuanced, but the patient can't intuit why their status is what it is.

VCA judgment: The AI synthesizes all data including trends — a patient whose pain is moderate but improving might still be "on track." Most sophisticated, but hardest to explain.

Pattern-based: Specific dimension combinations trigger specific tiers. High pain + poor sleep = yellow. High pain + missed meds = red. Rules-based, auditable for FDA.
